PRIMARY HEALTH CARE UNIT IN LOLIM

Fruits Ministries is operating a health care center at Lolim,serving the people in 23 villages with a popullation of about 15000 people. This health project was a request from the community and it was considered because of a death an expectant mother in her labor but the child survived and she died.

CURRENT CHALLENGES

  1. Only one Ward Room is used for short admissions for both sexes.

  2. The available mattresses used for admissions are already warn out,so needs replacement.

  3. No mosquito nets for patients and newborn babies.

  4. No light for night emergencies.

  5. Some drugs are lacking in the health center.

  6. Limited funding to increase the number of the health staff.

  7. No vehicle for medical emergencies.
