CHILDREN'S HOME

Location: Narus

BETHSAIDA CHILDREN'S HOME

Bethsaida children's home in Narus provides care,support and protection for seventy six (76) vulnerable children. The home has three (3) care takers, one cook,one attendant, one watchman, a clinic stationed in our health center is conducting check-up and providing medicines.
All children at Bethsaida are being provided three (3) times nutritious food, one set of books and four sets of uniforms and regular dresses.

CURRENT CHALLENGES

  • A Hall for children to study is constructed but not equiped with lights and sitting facilities.
  • Children's rooms have no lights at night.
  • Provided mosquito nets are now old,need a new replacements.
  • Lack of transport to bring in food items from the market. The available lorry is broken down.
  • Limited provisions of sanitary materials.
  • limited support given to the matron and cooks.
